Who are we?

What:

A non-profit organization whose goal is to provide children in the Revere Community with adequate food on weekends.

When:

Each weekend when school is in session.

Who:

Parents/guardians of children who are eligible for free or reduced lunch have the option to “Opt In.” The program began in September, 2017 at Richfield Elementary and expanded to Bath Elementary in February, 2018. In the fall of 2018, the program began to serve all of the schools in the Revere Local School District.

How:

Two or three breakfasts, two snacks, and two entree items are packed by members of The National Honor Society each school week based upon a preset menu. The food is discretely distributed to the children. Neither the Revere Power Pack Program nor the members of National Honor Society know the recipients’ names.

Why:

No child should be concerned about having enough food. Events sometimes change circumstances and finances within a household and, as a community, we need to be prepared to assist when needed.
