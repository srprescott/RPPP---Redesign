Monetary Donations

Monetary donations are particularly helpful as our storage space is limited and we may purchase what we need and when we need it. In addition, it allows us to purchase the specific items we need in quantities that we need. It also enables us to do some bulk purchasing.

Your tax deductible monetary donation should be made payable to the Revere Power Pack Program and may be mailed or delivered to at S&T Bank in Bath at 491 North Cleveland Massillon Rd. Akron, Ohio 44333. You will receive a letter acknowledging your donation.

Donate Food

We have a specific menu from which we are selecting items. If you wish to donate food or have a food drive on our behalf, please contact The Revere Power Pack Program at reverepowerpack@gmail.com, contact Jill O'Hara (330) 322-6632, or contact Hillcrest Elementary (330) 523-3603.

Donate An Auction Item

We are looking for items for our auction on February 9, 2019. Items, tickets, packages, are all welcomed. If you have something that you would like to donate, please email us at reverepowerpack@gmail.com or call Jill O’Hara at (330) 322-6632.
Your donation is tax deductible.
